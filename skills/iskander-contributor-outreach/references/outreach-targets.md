# Outreach Targets

Organisations with mission alignment to Iskander. For each, the key contact channel and what to emphasise.

## Tier 1: Direct Alignment (contact first)

### CoTech — Cooperative Technologists (UK)
- **URL**: https://www.coops.tech/
- **Contact**: contact@coops.tech
- **What they are**: UK network of ~40 worker-owned tech co-ops. They already use Loomio internally.
- **Why aligned**: They ARE the target user. Tech co-ops that need democratic tools and understand self-hosting.
- **Emphasise**: Loomio integration, self-hosted K3s stack, one-command install, federation between co-ops.
- **Ask**: Would any CoTech members test Iskander? Feedback on the architecture?

### Loomio
- **URL**: https://www.loomio.com/
- **Contact**: Via GitHub (loomio/loomio) or their contact page
- **What they are**: The democratic decision-making platform Iskander builds on.
- **Why aligned**: Iskander extends Loomio with AI, web3 anchoring, and federation. Symbiotic relationship.
- **Emphasise**: The Clerk agent as an accessibility layer for Loomio. Decision anchoring to IPFS. Not competing — extending.
- **Ask**: API guidance, potential partnership, feedback on the loomio-bridge skill architecture.

### DisCO.coop — Distributed Cooperative Organizations
- **URL**: https://disco.coop/
- **Contact**: Via their website or Guerrilla Translation
- **What they are**: Feminist economics framework for cooperatives. Value tracking across livelihood, love, and pro-bono work.
- **Why aligned**: Iskander explicitly adopts DisCO's three value streams in the Clerk agent design. Cited in whitepaper.
- **Emphasise**: How the Clerk tracks love work and pro-bono contributions alongside productive output. DisCO's direct influence on the design.
- **Ask**: Feedback on implementation of DisCO principles. Potential funding collaboration (they've received Interledger grants before).

### Radical Routes
- **URL**: https://www.radicalroutes.org.uk/
- **Contact**: Via their website
- **What they are**: UK network of housing and worker co-ops committed to radical social change.
- **Why aligned**: Housing and worker co-ops are primary Iskander users. They coordinate across a federation — exactly the use case.
- **Emphasise**: Federation protocol, self-hosting on cheap hardware, privacy (lunarpunk), democratic governance.
- **Ask**: Would any member co-ops pilot Iskander? Real-world governance needs?

## Tier 2: Strong Alignment (contact second)

### Platform Cooperativism Consortium (PCC)
- **URL**: https://platform.coop/
- **Contact**: Via The New School, NYC
- **What they are**: Research and advocacy hub for platform co-ops. Academic credibility.
- **Why aligned**: Iskander is platform cooperative infrastructure. PCC connects to hundreds of co-ops globally.
- **Emphasise**: The anti-extractive stance (no token speculation), ICA principle alignment, academic rigour of the whitepaper.
- **Ask**: Research partnership, visibility in their network, conference speaking.

### Co-operatives UK
- **URL**: https://www.uk.coop/
- **Contact**: Via their website
- **What they are**: National apex body for co-ops in the UK. Policy, advocacy, support.
- **Why aligned**: They promote the ICA principles Iskander implements. Access to thousands of UK co-ops.
- **Emphasise**: ICA principle compliance table from the whitepaper. Practical tools, not ideology.
- **Ask**: Visibility, newsletter feature, connection to co-ops that might test.
