# ICA Reference Documents Index

Foundational knowledge for Iskander's cooperative governance, agent design, and values assessment.

## ICA Guidance Notes to the Co-operative Principles (2015)

Source: International Co-operative Alliance Principles Committee, 2015.

Detailed guidance on the practical application of the 7 Cooperative Principles in contemporary terms for the 21st century. 120 pages of interpretation, examples, and recommendations.

## Co-ops for 2030 (2017)

Source: International Co-operative Alliance, "Co-ops for 2030: A Movement Achieving Sustainable Development for All", Annual Report Volume I, 2017.

Maps cooperative contributions to all 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Documents how cooperatives worldwide are addressing poverty, hunger, health, education, equality, climate, and governance.

## ICA Statement on the Cooperative Identity

Three components:
1. **Definition** — What a cooperative is
2. **Values** — 6 core values + 4 ethical values
3. **Principles** — 7 guidelines for putting values into practice
