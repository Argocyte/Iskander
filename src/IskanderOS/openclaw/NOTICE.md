For NLnet evaluators, security auditors, and any third-party reviewer assessing
Iskander's dependency posture:

   cooperative is working to address. The README and this NOTICE represent

2. **Iskander welcomes legal advice** from cooperative-tech lawyers, IP
   lawyers, or anyone with relevant expertise. Co-operatives UK membership
   (planned per the funding-research strategy) is the cooperative's intended
   path to legal commons access.

   careful observation and selective non-engagement: Iskander does not adopt

   the ecosystem context (this NOTICE) is intentional rather than evasive.
4. **Iskander's commitment to ICA ethical openness** means this NOTICE is a
   feature of the cooperative's transparency commitment, not a bureaucratic
   add-on. The cooperative's stance is that auditable disclosure beats
   silent assumption.
